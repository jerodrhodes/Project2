Take a look around!

The dashboard shares data provided by OPENDATA KC and the Census Bureau. The data includes counts on COVID-19 and crimes in Kansas City. We hope that you enjoy looking through our interactive visualizations. There is a map broken down by zip codes that show counts for COVID-19 cases and population. The second is a scatter plot of COVID-19 cases compared to the amount of crimes reported per zip code. The third is an interactive bar chart that displays the top ten zip codes with COVID-19 cases reported and how they relate to crime and population.
